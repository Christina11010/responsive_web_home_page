Please complete Soil Declaration Form before unloading any material on the site

The gate rate for clean fill is $8.00 + GST per cubic meter.

Please note that we do NOT accept materials as follow:

  • Building waste
  • Green waste
  • Rubbish
  • Polluted materials

If any of these items are found, the contractor will be responsible for their removal at their own cost.

For material originating from high-risk locations, such as factories or commercial properties, prior approval is necessary, and testing will be required before unloading on site to comply with the EPA guidelines.

Please do not hesitate to call 0123 456 789 if you require assistance or have any questions regarding the site.
